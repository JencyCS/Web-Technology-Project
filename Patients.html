Registration Process


1. Visit the Hospital Website: Access the "Patient Registration" section.

2. Complete the Registration Form: Provide personal information, address, insurance details, and medical history.

3. Submit Identification: Upload a valid ID and insurance card if applicable.

4. Review and Confirm: Check the accuracy of your information and agree to the privacy policy.

5. Submit Registration: Click the "Submit" button to finalize your registration.

6. Receive Confirmation: Get a confirmation email or message with further instructions.

7. Schedule an Appointment: Use the patient portal to book an appointment with a healthcare provider.

Insurance & Billing


Insurance Information: Provide and verify insurance details during registration.

Billing Process: Charges are based on services provided, and you will receive an itemized bill.

Payment Options: Pay in-person, online, or through insurance billing; pay any remaining balance.

Financial Assistance:Contact the financial assistance office for support with payments.

Contact Information: Reach out to the billing department for inquiries or issues.

Claims and Appeals: The hospital handles insurance claims and assists with appeals if needed.

Patient Portal


Accessing the Portal: Visit our hospital’s website and click on the "Patient Portal" link. Log in using your credentials or create a new account if you’re a first-time user.

View Medical Records: Access and review your medical history, test results, and visit summaries.

Appointment Management: Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments. View upcoming appointments and appointment history.

Billing and Payments: View your billing statements, make payments, and check the status of insurance claims.

Prescription Management: Request prescription refills and view your current medications.

Communicate with Healthcare Providers: Send and receive secure messages from your healthcare team. Ask questions or get follow-up information.

Update Personal Information: Update your contact details, insurance information, and other personal data.

Support and Help: Access help guides or contact support for assistance with portal issues.
